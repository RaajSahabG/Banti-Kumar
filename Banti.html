About Me

Professional Background

With over 10 years of experience in computer education, I've dedicated my career to empowering students with essential digital skills. As the founder and director of multiple computer education centers across Agra, I've helped thousands of students build successful careers in technology.

Beyond education, I serve as the Vice President of the Anti Corruption & Crime Control Committee in Agra, where I work diligently to promote transparency and ethical practices in our community.

My teaching philosophy centers on practical, hands-on learning that prepares students for real-world challenges. I believe in creating inclusive learning environments where students of all backgrounds can thrive.

Anti Corruption & Crime Control Committee

As the Vice President of the Anti Corruption & Crime Control Committee in Agra, I lead initiatives to promote transparency, accountability, and ethical practices across public and private sectors.

Our committee works closely with local government bodies, educational institutions, and community organizations to raise awareness about corruption and implement preventive measures.

Key achievements include establishing an anonymous reporting system, conducting awareness workshops in schools and colleges, and successfully mediating several high-profile corruption cases.
